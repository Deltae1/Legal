## EVIDENCE BACKUP CHAIN

Every piece of evidence follows this chain:

```
CAPTURE → PHONE → PROTON DRIVE → ENCRYPTED EXTERNAL DRIVE → ATTORNEY COPY
```

- **Never skip steps** — if glasses footage only exists on the glasses, it can be lost
- **ProtonDrive is your cloud backup** — not iCloud, not Google
- **Encrypted external drive** — keep at a separate location from your phone
- **Attorney copy** — once you have representation, everything goes to them

## SIGNAL QUICK REFERENCE

When something happens, ask these four questions:

1. **Focus?** — Is this grabbing my attention toward a specific idea or action?
2. **Authority?** — Is this wrapped in legitimacy (government, family, community leaders)?
3. **Tribe?** — Is this coming from my own people, building, or community?
4. **Emotional?** — Is this pulling on pride, fear, belonging, love, or nostalgia?

If yes to 2 or more → log it immediately. It's part of the pattern.
