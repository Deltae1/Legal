## Notes on Who to Send This To

**Best fit attorneys — look for these specialties:**
- Federal employment law (Title VII, ADA, FMLA)
- Whistleblower / retaliation (federal)
- Civil rights (Bivens claims)
- Federal contracts (for the T4C settlement component)

**Firms to target:**
- Washington DC firms with federal employment practice groups
- Government Accountability Project (whistleblower specialists)
- National Employment Law Project
- ACLU — if Bivens/constitutional angle is emphasized
- Firms that have handled USAGM or VOA cases before

**Contingency vs. hourly:**
- Strong documented cases like this are more likely to attract contingency representation
- Lead with the court ruling and the document stack — attorneys take contingency cases they believe they can win
- The W-2s + FMLA/accommodation approvals + court ruling = high-confidence case on paper
