## Tips for Attorney Outreach

1. **Always call first** — emails get buried; a phone call gets you a real conversation
2. **Lead with the clearest claim** — constructive discharge + hospitalization is your most concrete hook
3. **Mention medical records immediately** — attorneys respond to documented evidence
4. **Ask specifically:** "Do you handle federal agency employment retaliation cases?"
5. **If they say no** — ask for a referral to someone who does
6. **Follow up every 5 business days** if no response
7. **Keep a log** of who you contacted, when, and what they said (use ATTORNEYS/OUTREACH_LOG.md)
