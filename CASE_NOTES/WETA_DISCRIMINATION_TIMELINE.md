## Overview

Elvin Sangano was employed as a Technical Director at WETA Greater Washington
from February 14, 2022 through June 2024. Over that period he was subjected to
a continuous pattern of race discrimination that took four distinct but connected
forms:

1. **Probation sabotage** — white peer interference from day one
2. **Discriminatory job assignments** — two years of Audio assignments while white TDs received TD work
3. **Hostile work environment** — escalating coordinated harassment leading to constructive discharge
4. **Reinstatement denial** — denied return while a white comparator was reinstated by the same decision-maker

These are not isolated incidents. They are a single unbroken pattern of racial
discrimination from the first day of employment to the final lockout.

### February – May 2022 — Probation Sabotage

**What happened:**
- During probation, **Jay Naval** — a white TD peer with **no supervisory authority** over Elvin — told **Heather** (Tech Production Manager) and **Jim Schnider** (Director of Operations) that Elvin was *"not a good enough TD"*
- Based solely on Jay Naval's opinion, Elvin's probation was **extended by 3 months**
- No independent performance assessment was conducted
- No documented performance deficiency existed
- Elvin was given the opportunity to TD — and **delivered**
- After he proved himself, the probation issue was dropped with no further mention

**Probation ended: approximately May 2022**

**Legal significance:**
- First adverse employment action — occurred before Elvin had any opportunity to establish himself
- Jay Naval had zero authority to evaluate Elvin; he was a lateral peer
- Management (Heather + Schnider) accepted a white peer's subjective, unsubstantiated opinion over their own direct observation of a Black employee
- The probation extension was based on race, not performance
- Pattern established from day one: white peer input shapes management decisions about Black employee

**Key actors:**
| Name | Role | Conduct |
|---|---|---|
| Jay Naval | White TD peer | Told management Elvin was "not good enough" — no authority to do so |
| Heather | Tech Production Manager | Acted on Jay Naval's opinion without independent verification |
| Jim Schnider | Director of Operations | Sanctioned 3-month probation extension based on peer opinion |

### May 2022 – June 2024 — Two Years of Discriminatory Job Assignments

**What happened:**
- Following probation, Elvin was consistently assigned to **Audio** work
- White TDs — **Brian Smith**, **Steve Shaw**, and **Jay Naval** — received **TD assignments**
- Elvin was hired as a Technical Director and was fully qualified to perform TD work
- He had just demonstrated his TD capability during the probation challenge
- The pattern persisted for the entire remainder of his employment — approximately **two years**

**All assignments were distributed in writing by Nikki (scheduling coordinator)
via email. Every discriminatory assignment is documented and discoverable.**

**Legal significance:**
- Discriminatory job assignment = adverse employment action under Title VII and DCHRA
- Denial of opportunity to perform your job title while white colleagues perform it = race discrimination
- Two years of written scheduling records = documentary proof of the pattern
- Three named white comparators in the same role receiving preferential treatment
- Pattern shows the discrimination was structural and ongoing — not isolated

**Key actors:**

### Late June – Early July 2024 — Reinstatement Denied; Systems Lockout

**What happened:**
1. Elvin returned to DC from California — late June / early July 2024
2. Elvin contacted Jim Schnider via **Microsoft Teams**: *"Hi, do you have a moment"* — brief, professional, non-confrontational
3. **Same day:** Elvin was locked out of all WETA systems (email + Teams)
4. Elvin followed up — Schnider **removed Elvin from the WETA email server entirely**

**The comparator — Anthony DeRubio:**

|---|---|---|
| Race | White | Black |
| Resignation type | Voluntary | Involuntary (constructive discharge) |
| Resigned | December 2023 | June 2024 |
| Reinstated / denied | ✅ Reinstated March 2024 | ❌ Denied |
| Post-reinstatement role | Allowed to TD; later reassigned to EIC due to documented on-air errors | Never given the chance — denied reinstatement entirely |
| Circumstances at resignation | No documented duress | Hospitalized same week |
| Decision-maker | Jim Schnider | Jim Schnider |

**Jim Schnider approved DeRubio's return and denied Elvin's — same person, same role, radically different outcomes based on race.**

**Why the EIC reassignment strengthens the case:**
- DeRubio was reinstated, allowed to TD, then reassigned to EIC **because he made on-air errors**
- Elvin was never given the chance to return at all — despite having a clean performance record and no documented on-air errors
- WETA accommodated DeRubio's documented performance failures with a role adjustment
- WETA gave Elvin nothing — not reinstatement, not a reduced role, not any form of accommodation
- The white employee with actual documented errors was treated better than the Black employee with no documented errors
- This is the disparate treatment argument in its clearest possible form: **DeRubio failed and was accommodated; Elvin succeeded and was rejected**

**DeRubio–Usher connection:**
- Anthony DeRubio's cousin **Joe Usher** worked at VOA
- Usher is believed to have shared Elvin's schedule and assignment information with DeRubio
- This gave the WETA hostile environment network advance knowledge of Elvin's movements and work profile at VOA
- DeRubio is both the key racial comparator AND a possible participant in the coordination network

**Significance of same-day lockout:**
- Schnider's response to a casual *"Hi, do you have a moment"* was to immediately lock Elvin out of all systems
- This is not routine offboarding — offboarding happens at separation, not weeks later in response to a Teams message
- The disproportionate response demonstrates **consciousness of guilt** — Schnider knew why Elvin was reaching out
- Removing Elvin from the email server entirely after follow-up = deliberate destruction of communication avenue
