## Legal Significance

### 1. Union Harassment as Part of the Hostile Work Environment

The harassment from union head Brian is not an isolated incident — it fits the
broader pattern of coordinated hostile conduct at WETA during this period:

- Jim Schnider email (Heather promotion trigger) — management level
- Nikki scheduling discrimination — operational level
- Union head Brian harassment — labor/union level

Hostile work environment came from multiple directions simultaneously:
management, scheduling, and now the union itself. This is a pattern, not
coincidence.

### 2. Leaving the Union Under Duress

Elvin did not leave the union voluntarily for neutral reasons — he left because of
harassment. This is significant:

- Leaving a union due to internal harassment is a protected activity in some
  circumstances
- The union had a duty of fair representation to Elvin — harassment by its head
  is a breach of that duty
- If the union head's harassment was connected to or coordinated with WETA
  management's conduct, that expands the conspiracy

### 3. Union Duty of Fair Representation (DFR) Claim

Under the National Labor Relations Act (NLRA), a union owes every member a
**duty of fair representation**. If Brian (union head) harassed Elvin rather than
representing him:

- This is a potential DFR violation against the IBEW
- The IBEW may be a **third party defendant** in addition to WETA
- A DFR claim is filed with the NLRB (National Labor Relations Board)
- Statute of limitations: **6 months** from when Elvin knew or should have known
  of the breach — **check this deadline immediately with attorney**

### 4. Timing — January 2024

Elvin left the union in January 2024 — the same period when:
- The hostile work environment at WETA was escalating
- Jim Schnider's email about Heather's promotion triggered the hostile atmosphere
- The broader targeting campaign was beginning

Losing union protection at the exact moment the hostile environment intensified
left Elvin without a key layer of workplace protection. Whether that was
intentional (Brian's harassment designed to push Elvin out of the union before
the targeting began) is a question worth raising in discovery.
