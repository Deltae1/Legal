## Legal Significance

### 1. Brian's Dual Role — Union Head AND TD

Brian was not merely a union official who happened to harass Elvin. He was:
- **IBEW union head** — the person whose job was to protect Elvin
- **Technical Director** — received preferential TD assignments over Elvin
- **Ringleader** of the coordinated hostile environment at WETA

The same person who:
- Was supposed to represent Elvin through the union
- Benefited from discriminatory scheduling that kept Elvin off TD work
- Led the personal harassment and intimidation campaign against Elvin

That is a conflict of interest on top of a hostile environment claim on top of a DFR violation.

### 2. Harassment Continued After Resignation

Brian's conduct did not stop when Elvin resigned from the union. He continued
following, intimidating, and harassing Elvin through January 2024.

This proves the harassment was **personal and coordinated** — not a union
jurisdictional dispute. The union was a tool, not the motive.

### 3. Union Duty of Fair Representation (DFR) Claim

Under the NLRA, a union owes every member a **duty of fair representation**.
Brian (union head) harassing the member he was supposed to represent is the
most egregious possible DFR violation.

- DFR claim filed with the **NLRB (National Labor Relations Board)**
- Statute of limitations: **6 months** from the breach
- Breach occurred: December 2023 — January 2024
- **⚠️ This deadline may have already passed — confirm with attorney immediately**

### 4. Stripped of Protection at the Worst Moment

Elvin lost union protection in December 2023 / January 2024 — exactly when:
- The hostile work environment at WETA was escalating
- The broader targeting campaign was beginning
- He was most vulnerable and most in need of representation

Whether this was by design (push him out before the campaign intensifies) is
a question for discovery. The effect was the same regardless of intent.

### 5. The Coordination Network at WETA

The hostile environment came from multiple simultaneous directions:

| Level | Actor | Conduct |
|---|---|---|
| Management | Jim Schnider | Triggering email; hostile atmosphere |
| Union | Brian (head + TD) | Stalking, intimidation, ringleader |
| Union | Geoff | Coordinated post-resignation response |
| Operations | Nikki | Discriminatory scheduling (separate thread) |
| Staff | Tom (audio) | Foreknowledge of Schnider email — parking lot |

**This is an organized group — not a series of coincidences.**
